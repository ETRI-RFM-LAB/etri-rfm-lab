ABOUT ETRI RFM LAB

Next-Generation Robots
That Learn and Act

ETRI RFM Lab develops robot foundation models that unify perception, reasoning, and action using large-scale multimodal data.

  • Multimodal Robot Foundation Models

CORE RESEARCH

Robot Foundation Models

Our research spans foundation models for general-purpose robot intelligence and their deployment on real humanoid systems.

RFM

Multimodal Foundation Models

We learn unified representations of vision, language, robot states, and actions.

VLA

Vision-Language-Action

We generate executable robot actions from natural-language instructions and complex scenes.

We build pipelines that continually improve robot capabilities through real-world experience.
